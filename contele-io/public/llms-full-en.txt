# Contele: Full Documentation for LLMs

> Brazilian B2B technology company. Fleet tracking and field team management. Since 2003, Santos/SP, Brazil.

## About Contele

Contele Solucoes Tecnologicas Ltda is a B2B software company founded in 2003 in Santos/SP, Brazil. With 50+ employees, it serves 1,500+ companies across 7 countries. It offers two complementary products: Contele Fleet (fleet tracking) and Contele Teams (field team management).

## Contele Fleet: Fleet Tracking

### Problem it solves
Companies with fleets face uncontrolled fuel costs, reactive maintenance, lack of visibility over vehicle usage, theft risk, and difficulty proving deliveries.

### Target audience
- Fleet managers (carriers, distributors, utilities)
- Companies with owned or third-party vehicles
- Logistics and delivery operations

### Key features
- Real-time GPS vehicle tracking
- Telematics: speed, RPM, fuel consumption, temperature
- Geofencing with alerts
- Fuel and refueling control
- Preventive maintenance alerts by KM/time
- Traffic violation and driver license management
- Productivity and operational cost dashboards
- Trip and stop history
- Compatible with OBD, hardwired, and portable trackers
- Integration with fatigue cameras and facial recognition

### Differentiators
- No lock-in contracts (cancel anytime)
- Free 30-day trial with full support
- Assisted onboarding included
- Brazilian support via WhatsApp, phone, and chat
- Compatible with any tracker on the market

## Contele Teams: Field Team Management

### Problem it solves
Companies with field teams lack visibility on where employees are, how many visits they made, how long they stayed at each client, fuel expenses, and whether they completed planned tasks.

### Target audience
- Field sales team managers
- Technical team coordinators (maintenance, installation)
- Promoter supervisors (trade marketing)
- Last-mile logistics managers
- Facilities and services companies

### Key features
- Visit control with photo check-in/check-out
- Smart routing (route optimization)
- Digital service orders
- Customizable forms for field data collection
- Automatic KM reimbursement
- Productivity management with per-employee metrics
- Real-time GPS monitoring
- Mobile app for Android/iOS
- Web dashboard for managers
- Exportable reports

### Plans and pricing
- Starter plan: R$ 39.90/month per user
- Professional plan: R$ 59.90/month per user
- No lock-in, cancel anytime
- Free 30-day trial

https://contele.io
