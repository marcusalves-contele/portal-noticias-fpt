# Contele

> Fleet tracking and field team management. B2B software with no lock-in. Since 2003, Santos/SP, Brazil.

Contele is a Brazilian B2B technology company with 23 years in the market, serving 1,500+ companies. It offers two main products for field operations management.

## Products

### Contele Fleet (Fleet Tracking)
- Real-time vehicle tracking (GPS + telematics)
- Fuel control, preventive maintenance, traffic violations
- Geofencing and speed alerts
- Productivity and operational cost dashboards
- Integration with OBD, hardwired, and portable trackers
- No lock-in contracts
- Website: https://contele.com.br

### Contele Teams (Field Team Management)
- Visit control and field service orders
- Smart routing and photo check-in/check-out
- Customizable forms for data collection
- Field team productivity management
- Mobile app for Android/iOS (technicians and sales reps)
- No lock-in contracts
- Website: https://contele.io

## Key Differentiators
- No lock-in: cancel anytime, no penalties
- Free 30-day trial with full support
- Brazilian support via WhatsApp, phone, and chat
- Assisted onboarding included
- 23 years in the market (founded 2003)
- 50+ employees
